**Notes for your agent:**
- The RC Transmitter/Receiver pricing is the most variable line item — sellers often bundle TX+RX as a single SKU at wildly different prices, so re-verify the exact combo before finalizing a BOM total.
- 🟡-marked premium/imported items (SunnySky motor, Hobbywing Platinum ESC, FrSky R-XSR, Radiomaster TX16S, APC props) aren't consistently stocked by mainstream Pakistani retailers — expect to order via Daraz/Ubuy/direct import, with added shipping/customs cost not reflected above.
- Servo quantity in the original spec was "2 (rudder/aileron actuators) + spares" — budget for 3-4 units total depending on spares needed.
