Formulas Reference
Wing Loading = Total Weight (oz) ÷ Wing Area (ft²)
Power-to-Weight = Motor Watts ÷ Total Weight (lb)
Thrust-to-Weight = Static Thrust (g) ÷ Total Weight (g)
Aspect Ratio = Wingspan² ÷ Wing Area
Stall Speed = √(2W ÷ (ρ·S·C_Lmax)), C_Lmax ≈ 1.35
Flight Time = (mAh ÷ (Avg_A × 1000)) × 60 × 0.8
Top Speed ≈ RPM × Pitch(in) × 60 / 63360 × 0.85
C-Rating Check: Cap(Ah) × C ≥ Motor Max Amps
